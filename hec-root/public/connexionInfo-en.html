How to open a session :

HEC

  1. Enter your HEC (employee, student or graduate) id number in the "Username" field.
  2. Enter your HEC password in the appropriate field. Your password is the same as the one used to access your HEC Montréal e-mail and agenda applications.

Guest (without HEC ID number)

  1. Type in your email address.
  2. Type in the password that was sent to you by email.
